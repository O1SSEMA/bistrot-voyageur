About Wanderlust Bistro

Where Culinary Art Meets Wanderlust

At Wanderlust Bistro, we believe that food and travel are the two greatest pleasures of life. Our restaurant combines the finest culinary experiences with personalized travel planning services.

Founded in 2020 by Chef Maria Rodriguez and travel enthusiast James Thompson, we've created a unique space where guests can savor internationally-inspired dishes while planning their next adventure. Our team of experienced chefs and travel consultants work together to bring you flavors from around the world and help you experience them in their native settings.
